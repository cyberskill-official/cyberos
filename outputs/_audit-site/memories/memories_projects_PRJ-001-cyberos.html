PRJ-001 — CyberOS

CyberOS is the founder's flagship internal initiative at CyberSkill JSC: a unified, AI-native operating system for the company that doubles as the seed of a Phase-4 commercial product. The project is led by the founder/CEO (Trịnh Thái Anh / Stephen Cheng) with the Engineering Lead as architectural co-pilot. As of 2026-05-04 the project is in pre-P0: the PRD and SRS are written and act as source of truth; no production code is shipping yet; CyberSkill continues to run on its existing fragmented stack (Notion, Slack/Zalo, Asana, HubSpot, Gmail, Excel-payroll, paper contracts).

The intended timeline is an 18–24 month arc divided into five gated phases (P0–P4). P0 (months 1–3) builds the foundations: AUTH, AI, MCP, OBS, CHAT, BRAIN, GENIE/CUO. The exit gate is "Slack/Zalo decommissioned, all 10 employees onboarded, BRAIN populated, founder signs the gate-readiness report." Each subsequent phase has a similar hard gate.

The project's success is measured by the north-star metric (% of routine actions performed by AI agents, weighted by minutes saved) and by the eight strategic goals G1–G8. Cost discipline is explicit: production infra ≤$380/month at internal P2 scale; ≤$2,200/month at 50-tenant scale.

Open questions known at start

OQ-05 (multi-modal Genie surface), OQ-06 (on-prem deployment SKU), OQ-07 (BYO-LLM for tenants) are deferred. They are tracked in the PRD; revisit at P3+.
