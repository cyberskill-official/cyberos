Non-goals — what CyberOS is NOT

These are explicit non-goals to forestall scope creep. Each item names something CyberOS will not become regardless of how the market evolves. Promotion to "actively avoided" or "considered" status requires founder + Lead Architect dual sign.

Not a generic SaaS to compete with Salesforce, HubSpot, Notion, Slack, or Linear at scale. The CRM / KB / PROJ / CHAT modules are sized for a 10-to-50-person company; horizontal feature parity is not pursued at any scale.

Not a horizontal AI assistant. Genie/CUO is not ChatGPT-with-a-mascot. CUO has a persistent persona, a fixed scope contract, organisational memory, and audit-grade behaviour. No bolted-on "general AI chat" surface.

Not for non-knowledge-work industries. Engineered for software / design / product consultancies and adjacent knowledge-work shops. Manufacturing, logistics, retail, healthcare provider chains, and field-service operations are out of scope.

Not a developer-tool replacement. IDEs, CI/CD, version control, monitoring, alerting, deployment are named integrations (GitHub, ArgoCD, Grafana). CyberOS observes and orchestrates; it does not replace the developer's daily tool surface.

Not a platform for autonomous external action. CUO never sends external communications, signs contracts, moves money, or acts on irreversible operations without an explicit human gate. Locked at the architecture level, not at the prompt level.

Not free, not open-source, not fully self-hostable for non-customers. Commercial software with a phased customer-self-host option for T3 enterprise tenants at P3+. Host shell, MCP gateway, and BRAIN substrate are proprietary; community editions are not on the roadmap.

Not the primary system of record for compensation, equity, or legal documents. The underlying authoritative records (signed contracts, statutory filings, KMS-secured payslips) live in legal jurisdictions and external archives. CyberOS provides the operational surface; the legal home is elsewhere.

Not a B2C product. No consumer features, no app stores, no individual sign-up. The smallest sellable unit is a tenant.

Not an AI agent marketplace. CyberOS will not host third-party agents acting on tenant data. MCP integration is one-directional: external clients can call CyberOS tools; CyberOS does not invite arbitrary external agents in.

Not a US-headquartered company. CyberSkill JSC stays Vietnamese-incorporated with Vietnamese cultural identity. The Singapore HoldCo flip at P3 entry is for fundraising and tax efficiency only; the centre of gravity stays in Ho Chi Minh City.

How to use this memory

When a feature request, partnership, or strategic suggestion conflicts with one of these non-goals, surface the conflict and ask whether the user wants to (a) drop the request, (b) carve out a documented exception with dual sign, or (c) actually move the non-goal — which is itself a strategic decision needing a DEC entry.
