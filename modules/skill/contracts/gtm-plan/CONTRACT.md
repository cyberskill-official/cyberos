# gtm-plan@1 — Annual go-to-market plan

An `gtm-plan@1` artefact authors the annual go-to-market plan — ICP definition, segmentation, channel strategy, pricing posture, sales motion (PLG / sales-led / hybrid), enablement plan, attainment + quota model. Per Winning by Design (Jacco van der Kooij) revenue-architecture framework + MEDDIC/MEDDPICC qualification + Predictable Revenue (Aaron Ross) for outbound; PLG playbook (OpenView) for product-led.

## Required sections (template.md H2 order)
1. ICP & segmentation
2. Channel strategy (sales-led / PLG / partner)
3. Pricing posture
4. Sales motion + qualification (MEDDIC / SPICED / Sandler / BANT)
5. Quota + attainment model
6. Enablement plan
7. KPIs & success metrics
8. Risks & open issues

## Citations
- Winning by Design (Jacco van der Kooij) revenue-architecture framework
- MEDDIC / MEDDPICC qualification (Jack Napoli)
- Predictable Revenue (Aaron Ross)
- OpenView PLG playbook

## KPI
- Attainment % of quota
- CAC payback months
- Win rate by segment
