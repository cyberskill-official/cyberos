# knowledge-pipeline@1 — Quarterly knowledge-asset codification pipeline

A `knowledge-pipeline@1` artefact authors the quarterly knowledge-asset codification pipeline — engagement-by-engagement candidate harvesting, codification queue, asset-publishing cadence, reuse metrics. Critical for consulting firms (CyberSkill specifically per C-Suite Reference §7) where IP/asset codification is the moat. Per Nonaka SECI knowledge-conversion + Wenger Communities of Practice + Davenport knowledge-management + ANSI Z39.19 taxonomy.

## Required sections (template.md H2 order)
1. Reporting period & scope (in-scope practices / accounts)
2. Engagement-by-engagement candidate harvest (per-engagement: candidate-count + asset-type)
3. Codification queue (per-asset: owner, due date, codification stage)
4. Published this period (per-asset: title, type, audience, distribution channel)
5. Reuse metrics (per-asset: downloads / cites / engagement-use-count)
6. Taxonomy + tagging hygiene
7. Backlog + next-quarter priorities
8. Open issues

## Citations
- Nonaka SECI knowledge-conversion model
- Wenger Communities of Practice
- Davenport "Working Knowledge"
- ANSI Z39.19 (Guidelines for the Construction, Format, and Management of Monolingual Controlled Vocabularies)
- McKinsey Knowledge Management practice (internal model)

## KPI
- Codification throughput (assets per quarter)
- Reuse rate (uses / asset)
- Time-to-codification (engagement-end → asset published)
