# regulatory-filing@1 — Regulatory submission

A `regulatory-filing@1` artefact authors a regulatory submission — SEC filing (10-K / 10-Q / 8-K), FDA submission (IND/NDA/PMA), EU AI Act conformity assessment, GDPR Art. 33 breach notification, Vietnam Decree 13/2023 data-protection registration, etc. Per the relevant regulator's structural mandate.

## Format
- Section ordering MUST match the regulator's template (SEC 10-K Items 1-15; FDA IND CTD modules; AI Act Annex IV; etc.).
- Each material assertion MUST be sourced from a primary record.

## Required sections (template.md H2 order — regulator-specific)
1. Filing identification (regulator / submitting entity / period / type)
2. Required structural sections per regulator (e.g. 10-K Items 1-15)
3. Material disclosures
4. Exhibits & cross-references
5. Sign-off & certifications

## Citations
- SEC Edgar filing standards (10-K Items 1-15 per Regulation S-K)
- FDA IND / NDA / PMA per 21 CFR
- EU AI Act Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 Annex IV
- GDPR Regulation (EU) 2016/679 Art. 33
- Vietnam Decree 13/2023 PDPD

## KPI
- Filing accepted on first submission
- Days to response from regulator
