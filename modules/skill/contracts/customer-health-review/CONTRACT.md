# customer-health-review@1 — Quarterly customer health review

A `customer-health-review@1` artefact authors a quarterly customer-health review across the active customer base — health score distribution, at-risk-account list, expansion-pipeline candidates, advocacy-pipeline (referrals + case studies), CS team utilization. Distinct from `churn-analysis` (backward-looking, only-churners) — this is forward-looking on ACTIVE customers. Per Gainsight + Catalyst CS Ops + TSIA Customer Success benchmarks.

## Required sections (template.md H2 order)
1. Reporting period & scope (in-scope segments / tiers)
2. Health-score distribution (green / yellow / red — by segment + tier)
3. At-risk account list (with intervention plan per account)
4. Expansion-pipeline candidates (green-rated, expansion-ready)
5. Advocacy-pipeline (referrals + case-study + reference candidates)
6. CS team utilization (per-CSM book size + engagement frequency)
7. Trend vs prior quarter (cohort movement: green → yellow → red transitions)
8. Recommendations
