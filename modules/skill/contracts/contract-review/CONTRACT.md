# contract-review@1 — Contract review against playbook

A `contract-review@1` artefact reviews an incoming third-party contract (MSA, NDA, DPA, SOW, etc.) against the organization's negotiation playbook. Output is a structured deviation log + recommended redlines + risk classification (GREEN/YELLOW/RED).

## Format
- Per ABA Model Contract Clauses guidance + ACC (Association of Corporate Counsel) playbook conventions.
- Each deviation flagged against a named playbook position with severity + redline + business-impact assessment.

## Required sections (template.md H2 order)
1. Document & parties
2. Counterparty position vs playbook
3. Deviation log
4. Recommended redlines
5. Risk classification (GREEN / YELLOW / RED)
6. Negotiation guidance
7. Escalation triggers

## Citations
- ABA Model Contract Clauses (Mergers & Acquisitions Committee)
- ACC Contract Playbook reference (Association of Corporate Counsel)
- WorldCC (formerly IACCM) contract benchmarking data

## KPI
- Contract cycle time
- Redline acceptance rate
- Risk-class accuracy (post-hoc audit)
