# delivery-review@1 — Quarterly delivery health review

A `delivery-review@1` artefact authors a COO-level quarterly delivery-health review — per-engagement status, utilization, margin, risks, customer satisfaction, capacity-vs-pipeline alignment. Per Bain agile-PMO + McKinsey delivery-excellence model + DORA metrics for engineering engagements.

## Required sections (template.md H2 order)
1. Reporting period & engagement scope
2. Per-engagement health (green/yellow/red traffic light + narrative)
3. Utilization roll-up (by role / by team / org-wide)
4. Margin analysis (gross / net per engagement)
5. Risks & escalations
6. Customer satisfaction (CSAT / NPS per engagement)
7. Capacity vs pipeline alignment
8. Decisions needed

## Citations
- Bain agile-PMO model
- McKinsey delivery-excellence framework
- DORA metrics (deployment frequency / lead time / change failure / MTTR) for engineering engagements
- TSIA (Technology Services Industry Association) services benchmarks

## KPI
- % engagements green
- Org utilization vs target
- Gross margin vs target
- Top engagement risks count
