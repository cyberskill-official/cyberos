# Late filing penalties — Nghị định 125/2020/NĐ-CP

## Authority

- **Nghị định 125/2020/NĐ-CP** — primary instrument. Article 13 sets the administrative-penalty bands. Article 8 covers mitigating / aggravating circumstances.
- **Luật Quản lý thuế 38/2019/QH14**, Article 59 — daily 0.03% interest on under-paid tax. This is *separate* from the administrative penalty and the two compound.
- **Bộ Luật Hình Sự 2015 (sửa đổi 2017)**, Article 200 — criminal tax evasion. Triggered for deliberate / sustained evasion typically above 100 million VND in evaded tax.

## Penalty bands (Article 13, ND 125/2020)

| Days late | Penalty band | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1–5 days | Warning (cảnh cáo) | No monetary penalty if mitigating circumstances are documented and the taxpayer has a clean compliance history. |
| 6–30 days | 2 000 000 – 5 000 000 VND | Standard band for short delays. |
| 31–60 days | 5 000 000 – 8 000 000 VND | Authority discretion within band based on facts. |
| 61–90 days | 8 000 000 – 15 000 000 VND | A formal written explanation (giải trình) is typically expected. |
| > 90 days | 15 000 000 – 25 000 000 VND | + Article 200 criminal investigation possible if intent to evade is shown and the amount is material. |

These bands apply per missed return. Multiple late returns are penalised separately and may aggravate the assessment for the latest one.

## Mitigating circumstances (Article 9, ND 125/2020)

The tax authority MAY reduce the assessment toward the low end of the band, or downgrade by one band, if the taxpayer demonstrates:

- Force majeure event (natural disaster, fire, accident) directly causing the delay.
- Serious illness / death affecting the responsible person, supported by official documentation.
- Voluntary disclosure before the authority initiated an investigation.
- Clean compliance history (no prior administrative penalties within the past 12 months).
- Full payment of the underlying tax + interest at the time of the late filing.

## Aggravating circumstances (Article 10)

Penalties escalate toward the high end of the band, or by one band, if:

- The taxpayer has been penalised for a similar violation within the past 12 months.
- The violation involves multiple periods or multiple tax types.
- The taxpayer obstructs the authority's investigation.

## Interest on under-paid tax (Article 59, Law 38/2019/QH14)

> Tiền chậm nộp được tính theo mức 0,03%/ngày tính trên số tiền thuế chậm nộp.

A daily 0.03% interest accrues on the unpaid tax amount, from the day after the original due date until the day of payment. This is independent of the administrative penalty and the two compound. A return that is filed late AND under-paid is therefore subject to:

1. The Article 13 administrative band (above).
2. Plus 0.03% × days_late × unpaid_principal interest.
3. Plus, if intent is shown, the Article 16 false-declaration penalty (20% of the under-paid amount) and possibly Article 200 criminal exposure.

## Criminal exposure (Article 200, Penal Code 2015 as amended 2017)

Tax evasion becomes a criminal matter when:

- The evaded amount is ≥ 100 million VND, AND
- Intent / wilful misconduct can be established (typically: forged invoices, sham transactions, repeated late filings with under-payment).

Penalties under Article 200:

- Fine of 100 million – 4.5 billion VND
- 3 months – 7 years imprisonment depending on amount evaded and aggravating factors

Late-filing alone (without under-payment) does NOT trigger Article 200. The criminal limb is reached only when (a) the return understates tax AND (b) intent is demonstrable.

## Voluntary disclosure (`khai bổ sung tự nguyện`)

A taxpayer who files a supplementary / corrective return (Mẫu 03/GTGT) BEFORE the authority initiates an examination receives substantial mitigation:

- The administrative penalty is reduced by one band (typically).
- The Article 16 false-declaration penalty (20%) does not apply.
- The Article 59 interest still accrues — it is not waivable.

Voluntary disclosure must be filed within 10 years of the original return's deadline; after 10 years the assessment authority is time-barred.

## Worked example

Taxpayer X files the May 2026 VAT return on 2026-07-15 (deadline `2026-06-22` after weekend roll-forward). Days late = 23. The return declares 12 000 000 VND payable, fully paid at filing.

- **Administrative band**: 6–30 days → 2 000 000 – 5 000 000 VND. Clean history → likely toward 2M.
- **Article 59 interest**: 0.03% × 23 × 12 000 000 = 82 800 VND.
- **Total estimated exposure**: ~ 2.08 million VND.

If the same return had been filed 95 days late instead: band jumps to 15 000 000 – 25 000 000 VND, interest scales to ~ 342 000 VND, and the authority MAY open an examination to assess whether the delay reflected intent.
